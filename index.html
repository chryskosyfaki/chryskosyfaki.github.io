Bio

I received my PhD degree in Computer Science in 2023 from the University of Ioannina, Greece supervised by Prof. Nikos Mamoulis. I am currently a post-doc researcher in Computer Science, at the University of Hong Kong, working with Prof. Reynold Cheng. I have published papers in top database conferences (EDBT, ICDE, SIGMOD, VLDB). I have also served on the program committees of international conferences on databases and data mining.

Research Interests

My research interests are in temporal data analytics, data management and temporal graph analytics.
