Bio

Hi! My name is Chrysanthi Kosyfaki. I received my bachelor degree from the Department of Computer Science, Ionian University and my master degree from Department of Computer Science and Engineering, University of Ioannina, Greece. Currently, I am a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, University of Ioannina, supervised by Prof. Nikos Mamoulis.

Research Interests

My research interests are in temporal data analytics, data management and temporal graph analytics.
